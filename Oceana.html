Oceana 

- Founded in October 2001 by actor and environmentalist Ted Danson
- The current CEO of Oceana is Andrew Sharpless
- As of 2017, Oceana had around 6000 volunteers and 200 staff members


    
Since Oceana's creation, they have protected nearly 4 million square miles of 
    ocean and have won 225 victories, including:
--> Winning habitat protections in the Strait of Sicily, June 2016
--> Protecting (alongside allies) deep-sea corals in the Gulf of Mexico, October 2020
--> Preventing (alongside Coastal communities and Businesses) harmful seismet 
    airgun blasting in U.S. Atlantic Waters, October 2020
