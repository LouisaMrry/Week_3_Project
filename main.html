Oceana



- Founded in October 2001 by actor and environmentalist Ted Danson
- The current CEO of Oceana is Andrew Sharpless
- As of 2017, Oceana had around 6000 volunteers and 200 staff members
Since Oceana's creation, they have protected nearly 4 million square miles of ocean and have won 225 victories, including:
--> Winning habitat protections in the Strait of Sicily, June 2016
--> Protecting (alongside allies) deep-sea corals in the Gulf of Mexico, October 2020
--> Preventing (alongside Coastal communities and Businesses) harmful seismet airgun blasting in U.S. Atlantic Waters, October 2020

Coral Reef Alliance



The Coral Reef Alliance strives to end the danger to coral reefs across the world. Working at all scales of coral protection, as one of the largest NGO's, CORAL uses specialist technology in coordination with community connections to help reduce threats and save the incredible ocean we all love.

The 5 Gyres Institute



- Founded in 2009
- Founded by Marcus and Anna Cummins
Impact:
- Discovered 5.25 trillion particles of plastic smog
- Diverted 16 billion microbeads from the ocean with the #beadfree pledge resulting in the Microbeads Free Waters Act of 2015
- Explored 50,000+ nautical miles on 19 research expeditions over 10 years
